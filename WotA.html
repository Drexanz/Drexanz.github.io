The War of the Abyss

A battle waged on the entire world by Vorago of the Abyss as a means of revenge on Zephyr, the God of Creation.

The war itself took place from 8 Vorian, 1545 SA to 10 Mundory, 1550 SA. However, Vorago's Chosen, Gait Terrene, had been planning his lord's revival from his original death on 17 Ustrious, 7 SA.
